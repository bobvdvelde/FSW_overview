Departments linked by common themes

In 2013, the FSW drafted a overview of experts for each department, together with the broad themes they were working on.
This provides some insight into the departments as they officially participated in the same themes: Departments by shared themes

Official themes and their links based on the departments that use them

Using the official roster of experts and their themes, we also drafted a network which links these themes based on their use within the same departments.
Clicking through the nodes, you can get a sense of which themes are used in which departments, and how limited the number of themes per department seems to be.
